# Guide for Students (Youth Members)

Welcome to the Student Guide. Here, you will find information on how to use the core features of the Catechist School application.

---

## 📲 QR Code Attendance

Attendance tracking is an essential part of your learning path. The app provides a personal QR code to check in at classes or events.

### How to get your personal QR code

1. Log in to your student account.
2. On your dashboard or profile page, you will see your QR code displayed at the top.
3. Take a screenshot or save this QR code to your mobile device for quick scanning at class.

### Scanning attendance

- When arriving at class, present your QR code to the designated Catechist.
- The Catechist will scan your QR code using their phone or device scanner.
- You will immediately receive a successful attendance notification in the app.

---

## 📊 View Grades & Comments

You can track your academic progress and read comments from your Catechists.

### View grades

1. Click on the **Grades** or **Academic** menu.
2. Here, your grades for various exams (Oral, 15-minute, 1-hour, Term Exam) will be displayed per semester.

### View comments

- Catechists write reviews on your study attitude, attendance, and behavior at the end of each semester or major event.
- Click on your class details to read encouraging words and advice from your teachers.

---

## 📅 Class Schedule & Events

Stay updated with class schedules and troop events.

- **Regular schedule**: See class hours, room numbers, and assigned Catechists.
- **Liturgical & Special events**: Check details for holy days, outdoor activities, camping, or retreats.
