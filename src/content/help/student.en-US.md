# Guide for Students (Youth Members)

Welcome to the Student Guide. Here, you will find information on how to use the core features of the Catechist School application.

---

## 🏠 Dashboard & Profile

After logging in, your account only has two menu items: **Dashboard** and **Help**.

- **Dashboard**: Shows your current class, enrollment info, and a summary of your attendance and grades for the active academic year.
- **Profile**: Click your avatar/name to open your full profile, showing your complete history across academic years.

---

## 📲 Attendance

- Attendance at class or Mass is recorded by your Catechist, either by scanning a QR code (generated for you and shown by staff) or by manual roll call.
- **Students do not have a personal QR code to display themselves** — the QR code is generated and shown on the Catechist/Admin side only.
- Attendance states you may see: **Present**, **Late**, **Excused Absence**, **Unexcused Absence**.
- You can review your own attendance history on your Dashboard or Profile page.

---

## 📊 Grades & Comments

You can track your academic progress and read comments from your Catechists on your **Dashboard** or **Profile** page.

### Grade types

- Scores are recorded per Short Quiz, Midterm Test, and Semester Exam, on a scale of 0 to 10.
- Grades are shown per semester alongside your average score.

### Comments

- Catechists record a conduct/morality rating and a note about your learning attitude, attendance, and behavior at the end of each semester.
- These appear on the same Dashboard/Profile summary as soon as your Catechist saves them.
