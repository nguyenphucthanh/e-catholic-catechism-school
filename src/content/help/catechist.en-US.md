# Guide for Catechists (Teachers)

Welcome to the Catechist Guide. Here, you will find information on managing classes, tracking attendance, and recording academic performance.

---

## 🏫 Class Management & Roster

As an assigned Catechist (either Homeroom or Co-teacher), you have access to the classes you are assigned to in the active academic year.

### Viewing class info

- Go to the **Classes** menu on the left sidebar.
- Select the class you wish to manage.
- You will see the student roster, total count, average attendance rate, and details of co-teachers.

---

## 📝 Taking Attendance

The app supports two attendance methods: QR code scanning (fastest) and manual roll call.

### QR Code attendance (Recommended)

1. On the class detail page, select the **Attendance** tab.
2. Select the current session (or create a new session).
3. Click the **Scan QR** button to launch your device camera.
4. Aim the camera at the QR code presented by the student. The system will instantly record their attendance.

### Manual attendance

- If a student doesn't have a QR code, you can manually mark them by checking the box next to their name.
- Attendance states include: **Present**, **Excused** (Vắng phép), or **Unexcused** (Vắng không phép).
- Be sure to click **Save** after finalizing the roll call.

---

## 📈 Inputting Grades & Term Evaluations

Assess student learning progress directly on the class interface.

### Entering grades

1. Choose the **Grades** tab in the class detail view.
2. Grade columns include: Oral, 15-minute, 1-hour, and Term Exam.
3. Double-click the respective grade cell for a student to type in the value (0 to 10).
4. The system automatically saves the input and computes the average score.

### Reviews & attendance assessment

- Fill in qualitative evaluations regarding the student's learning attitude and behavior.
- These scores and reviews will be visible on the student's profile account immediately.
