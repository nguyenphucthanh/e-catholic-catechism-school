# Guide for Catechists (Teachers)

Welcome to the Catechist Guide. Here, you will find information on managing classes, tracking attendance, and recording academic performance.

---

## 🏫 Class Management & Roster

As an assigned Catechist (either Homeroom or Co-teacher), you have access to the classes you are assigned to in the active academic year.

### Viewing class info

- Go to the **Classes** menu on the left sidebar to see the list of classes (name, branch, class type).
- Select a class to open its detail page.
- The detail page shows the student roster, total student count, and the homeroom/co-teacher assignments for that class.

---

## 📝 Taking Attendance

The app supports two attendance methods: QR code scanning (fastest) and manual entry.

### QR Code attendance (Recommended)

1. Open **QR Attendance** from the sidebar (a standalone page, separate from the class detail view).
2. Click **Scan QR** to launch your device camera.
3. Aim the camera at the QR code shown for the student. The system instantly records their attendance and shows a success/duplicate/unknown result.

### Manual attendance

- On a class's detail page, open the **Attendance** tab (Grid or Summary view).
- Click a student's cell for the session to open a small popover, pick their status, and save that entry.
- Attendance states: **Present**, **Late**, **Excused Absence** (Vắng phép), **Unexcused Absence** (Vắng không phép).

---

## 📈 Inputting Grades & Term Evaluations

Assess student learning progress on a class's **Exams** tab (Scores / Evaluations sub-tabs).

### Entering grades

1. Choose the **Exams** tab in the class detail view, then **Scores**.
2. Grade columns are named per exam, typically Short Quiz, Midterm Test, and Semester Exam.
3. Click a student's cell for a column to open a popover and type in the value (0 to 10).
4. Save the entry from the popover; the class average recalculates automatically.

### Reviews & attendance assessment

- Under the **Evaluations** sub-tab, fill in qualitative evaluations and a conduct/morality rating for the student's learning attitude and behavior.
- These scores and reviews are visible on the student's profile as soon as you save them.
